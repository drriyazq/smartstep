SmartStep Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Applies to: SmartStep Android application

SmartStep is a parent-facilitated life-skills app for children aged 5–16 and an independent learning app for adults 18+. This policy explains what information we handle, where it is stored, and your rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (India) and the Google Play Families Policy.

1. Who we are

SmartStep is published by the SmartStep team. You can reach us at drdentalmail@gmail.com for any privacy question, grievance, or data-rights request.

2. What information we handle

On this device (never uploaded)

We store the following on your phone only, using encrypted local storage (AES-256 via Android Keystore):

  • Your child's (or your own) name or nickname
  • Date of birth, sex, and living environment (urban / suburban / rural)
  • Skill progress — which skills have been practised, mastered, or skipped
  • Reward choices you select after completed skills
  • Custom tasks and rewards you add yourself
  • Parental consent confirmation and timestamp

Sent to our servers

  • The phone number you enter is sent to Google Firebase Authentication solely to verify that you are the real owner via OTP. We do not store your phone number anywhere outside that process.
  • Anonymous telemetry when a skill is completed: the skill's identifier, your child's age band (e.g. "7–8"), and your child's environment. This is used only to improve which skills are surfaced. It contains no name, phone number, child identifier, or precise date of birth.
  • Crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics) — release builds only — the device model, OS version, app version, and the stack trace of any crash. Contains no personal data.

Never collected

  • Location, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS content, or device storage
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Biometric data
  • Photos, recordings, or writings of your child

3. Why we handle this information

  1. To run the skill ladder on your device (all on-device data)
  2. To verify you are a real person during sign-in (phone OTP)
  3. To improve the app for all families (anonymous telemetry + crash diagnostics only)

4. How long we keep it

  • On-device data stays until you delete it. You can delete individual profiles, reset progress, or log out at any time from the Profile screen.
  • Anonymous telemetry is retained indefinitely in aggregate form, with no way to re-identify anyone.
  • Phone number during authentication is handled by Google Firebase under their terms. Firebase does not share it back to us as a usable identifier.
  • Crash reports are retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days by default.

5. Your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (India) and the Google Play Families Policy, you have the right to:

  • Access your (or your child's) data — use Export data in Profile to download a JSON file of everything stored on-device.
  • Correct any information — edit the profile or environment from Profile at any time.
  • Erase the data — delete individual profiles or log out to wipe everything.
  • Withdraw consent — logging out withdraws consent and erases all on-device data.
  • Raise a grievance — write to drdentalmail@gmail.com for any complaint. We acknowledge within 7 days and respond within 30 days.

6. Children's privacy

SmartStep is designed to be used by parents or guardians on behalf of children. No child creates an account or interacts with the app without an adult present. Parental consent is obtained explicitly before any child information is entered.

If you believe a child has set up the app without guardian consent, please contact us at drdentalmail@gmail.com and we will delete all associated data within 7 days.

7. Third-party services

SmartStep uses the following services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Google Firebase Authentication — to send OTP and verify your phone number
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — reserved for future use (e.g. weekly practice reminders). Not active yet.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — automatic crash diagnostics for release builds only
  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage aggregates (events, not identifiers)

No advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or data brokers are used. All Firebase services used are compliant with the Google Play Families Policy.

8. Security

  • All sensitive local data (profile, auth tokens, progress) is encrypted at rest using AES-256 with a key held in the Android Keystore.
  • Network traffic uses HTTPS with TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • The app is signed with a developer-controlled key; tampered APKs are rejected by Android.
  • We follow Google Play's Data Safety and Families Policy standards.

9. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the Last updated date at the top will change. Material changes will be announced in-app before they take effect.

10. Contact

  • The word Privacy in the subject line
  • Which profile the request concerns (if applicable)
  • The type of request (access / correct / delete / withdraw consent / grievance)

We acknowledge receipt within 7 days and respond fully within 30 days.
