## Full description (4000 characters max)

```
SmartStep is a parent-facilitated life skills app for children aged 5–16 — and an independent learning app for adults aged 18+ who want to get street-smart about real life.

Each skill is broken into one micro-task. When your child (or you) practise it, the next skill unlocks — like a game ladder. No ads. No tracking. No child data uploaded anywhere.

WHAT IS IT FOR?
• Children: learn the 400+ everyday skills school never teaches — how to handle money, cross a road safely, spot a scam, cook a simple meal, greet adults, say sorry properly, stay safe online, and much more.
• Adults: build the 70+ skills most people only pick up painfully by trial and error — from reading a payslip and building an emergency fund to having difficult conversations, handling bad bosses, filing an FIR, or managing your ageing parents' finances.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Consent and privacy come first. You review exactly what SmartStep holds and agree before any profile is created.
2. Set up a profile — for your child (5–16) or yourself (18+).
3. Take a short baseline check-in. Skills you already know get skipped automatically.
4. Get a personalised ladder across six categories: Financial, Household, Digital, Navigation, Thinking, and Social.
5. Tap any unlocked skill, read the how-to, practise it, and pick a reward when done.

SIX CATEGORIES OF SKILLS
• Financial — from coin recognition for little ones to budgeting, investing, insurance, and tax for adults
• Household — from clearing the plate to cooking a meal for ten, tenant rights, home repairs
• Digital — from safe app use for kids to recognising advanced phishing, identity theft response, and AI tool discipline for adults
• Navigation — from knowing home address to filing an FIR, international travel, and handling police stops
• Thinking — from following two-step instructions to using decision frameworks, weekly reviews, and side-project discipline
• Social — from saying please and thank you to giving feedback, resolving conflict, and repairing broken relationships

WHY IT WORKS
Most kids and adults learn life skills randomly — whatever life happens to throw at them, in whatever order. SmartStep gives you a curated ladder: master the foundations first, progress to harder skills only when you're ready. Each skill shows you exactly how to practise it, what to watch out for, and (for children) why it matters for development.

PRIVACY & SAFETY
Your child's name, date of birth, environment, and progress are stored ONLY on your device — encrypted with AES-256. We never upload this to any server. When a skill is completed we send only an anonymous count (age band + environment + skill ID) so we can improve the app. Nothing identifies your child.

You can export all of your child's data as JSON at any time. You can delete a single child or log out (which wipes everything) from Profile. We never sell data, never show ads, and never share data with third parties.

MADE FOR INDIAN FAMILIES (AND BEYOND)
SmartStep content is grounded in Indian contexts — EMI calculations, state-level policies, UPI payments, monsoon food storage — but every skill applies globally.

NO ADS. NO PURCHASES.
SmartStep is free and stays that way during the early phase. No ads interrupt your child. No in-app purchases. Just skills.

Built by parents, for families who want their children to grow up street-smart — and for adults who missed out on some of that growth and want to catch up now.
```
